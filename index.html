About Me

Frontend Designer with a designer’s eye and a developer’s mindset.
I’ve been part of Miracle Interface since 2011, where my journey began as a Graphic Designer and evolved into a Frontend Designer in 2014.

With formal training in Graphic Designing and Web Designing, I transformed my passion for visuals into interactive, user-friendly web experiences. Miracle Interface gave me the opportunity to grow from graphics to frontend, and today I work as a CSS / Frontend Designer, doing work I truly enjoy.

I’m happy in my role, continuously learning, and passionate about creating clean, functional, and visually engaging interfaces.
